Wetlands and EcoArt!

What are Wetlands?

A wetland is a distinct ecosystem that is flooded by water, either permanently or seasonally. Flooding results in oxygen-free processes prevailing, especially in the soils.

What is Ecoart?

Ecological art is an art genre and artistic practice that seeks to preserve, remediate and/or vitalize the life forms, resources and ecology of Earth.
